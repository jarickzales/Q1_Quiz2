Toy Story

Toy Story is an animated movie about toys who come alive when their owner, Andy, is not around.
Our main character in the movie is Woody. He feels scared when a new toy comes around named Buzz Lightyear.
He thinks that he will get replaced and Buzz will become Andy's new favorite toy.
They have to work together and try to become friends with each other.



The Main Characters
